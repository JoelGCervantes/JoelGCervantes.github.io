Projects

futAnalysis

FUT ANALYSIS is a CV tool that transforms match footage into live analytics. I fine-tuned a YOLO detector, used interpolation for stable ball tracking, clustered jersey colors with K-Means to separate teams, and overlaid metrics—possession, player speed/distance, and ball trajectories—directly on video.
